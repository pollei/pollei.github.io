This course is the study of the common framework and practices for applying project management practices in business. Topics include processes, interactions and common practices in the field of project management. Emphasis is placed on the knowledge area defined by the Project management Institute body of knowledge. This course will provide practical exercises and case studies. Prerequisite: Applied BAS degree students only. Lisa Zuber
